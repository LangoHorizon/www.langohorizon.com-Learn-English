Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

How do I know which course is right for me?

We offer a free placement test that assesses your current English level and recommends the most suitable course for your needs. You can take the test anytime on our website.

What's the difference between group and private lessons?

Group lessons are more affordable and provide opportunities to interact with other learners. Private lessons offer personalized attention and can be scheduled at your convenience. Many students combine both for optimal results.

Can I access the courses on my mobile device?

Yes! Our platform is fully responsive and works perfectly on smartphones and tablets. Since our classes are live, you can access it on any device and anywhere.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept direct payments through ria, and other payment types like PayPal, and bank transfers.

Do you offer certificates upon completion?

Yes, all our courses provide a certificate of completion. All of our English courses comes with an accredited certificate.
